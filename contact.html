Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a free estimate?

Simply fill out our contact form, call us, or send an email. We'll schedule a convenient time to visit your property and provide a detailed written estimate at no cost.

How long does a typical project take?

Project duration varies based on size and scope. A single room might take 1-2 days, while a whole house exterior could take 3-5 days. We'll provide a detailed timeline with your estimate.

Do you provide all materials?

Yes, we supply all necessary materials including premium paints, primers, brushes, rollers, and protective coverings. All materials are included in your estimate.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely! We are fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We can provide proof of insurance and licensing upon request for your peace of mind.

Do you offer a warranty?

Yes, we stand behind our work with a comprehensive warranty. Interior paint jobs are warranted for 2 years, and exterior work for 3 years against defects in workmanship.

Can you help with color selection?

Definitely! Our color consultants can help you choose the perfect colors for your space. We consider lighting, room function, and your personal style preferences.

Need Emergency Painting Services?

Water damage? Fire damage? We offer emergency painting services for urgent situations.
